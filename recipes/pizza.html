Delicious tomato with smoked sausage pizza

Here are the ingredients and the steps to make that pizza:

Ingredients
  • Chimichurry
  • One tomatoe
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Smoked Sausage
  • Cheese
  • Pizza Dough
Steps
  1. Spread the tomato sauce on top of the pizza dough
  2. CUt the tomato in thin slices
  3. Start to fry the smoked sausage
  4. Put cheese on top of the tomato sauce
  5. Start putting the tomato slices on top of everything
  6. Start putting the fried smoked sausage on top of it
  7. Put some chimichurry on top of the pizza
  8. Put it inside the oven for about 20 minutes
