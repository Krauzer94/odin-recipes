Delicious hamburguer recipe made with australian bread

Here are the ingredients and the steps to make that hamburguer:

Ingredients
  • Australian bread
  • Cheese
  • 300gr of red meat
  • Cabage
  • Tomato
  • Chimichurry
  • Cream cheese
Steps
  1. Cut the australian bread in half and put crem creese on it
  2. Start to pour some chimichurry on top of the red meat
  3. Fry the red meat for about 20 minutes
  4. Put cheese and cabage inside the bread
  5. Put the fried meat inside the bread
