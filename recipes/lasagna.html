Odin Recipes | Lasagna

Delicious hamburguer recipe made with australian bread

Required ingredients

  • Lasagna dough
  • Chimichurry
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • 300gr of red meat
  • White sauce
  • Onion
  • Tomato

Steps for preparation

  1. Cut the onion and tomato in tiny pieces
  2. Put them in the oven with the red meat
  3. Fry everything for about 30 minutes
  4. Start to build the lasagna using the dough
  5. Make one level of dough, and one level of white sauce
  6. Keep doing this until the white souce is completely used
  7. Put chimichurry in the meat that is being fried
  8. When the meat is done frying, put it inside the bread
